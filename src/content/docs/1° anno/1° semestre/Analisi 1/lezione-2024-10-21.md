$$
(a\ f(x_{0}) + b\ g(x_{0}))' = a\ f'(x_{0}) + b\ g'(x_{0})
$$

$$
(f(x_{0})\ g(x_{0}))' = f(x_{0})\ g'(x_{0}) + g({x_0})\ f'(x_{0})
$$

$$
